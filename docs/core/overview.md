# Overview

Ash Run is a single-player, turn-based tactics roguelite inspired by Advance Wars and Fire Emblem.

The game combines:

- Tactical grid-based combat
- On-map income and unit recruitment
- Persistent units with permadeath
- Roguelite progression across repeated runs
- A subtle 70s and 80s synthwave aesthetic

Players lead a commander through a straight sequence of battles, growing stronger during and between runs.

The core hybrid is important: players can buy fresh units during a map, but surviving units also carry forward through the run with their levels, XP, and earned stats intact.

The tone is grounded military with retro-futuristic flavor, avoiding excessive neon or over-the-top cyberpunk naming.

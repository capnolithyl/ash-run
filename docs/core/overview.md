# Overview

Iron Frontier: Neon Warpath is a **single-player, turn-based tactics roguelite** inspired by Advance Wars and Fire Emblem.

The game combines:

- Tactical grid-based combat
- Persistent units with permadeath
- Roguelite progression
- Subtle 70s/80s synthwave aesthetic

Players lead a commander through a sequence of battles, growing stronger during and between runs.

The tone is **grounded military with retro-futuristic flavor**, avoiding excessive neon or cyberpunk clichés.
